Introduction

Hello, my name is Fatta (he/him). I grew up in Surabaya, Indonesia, and am currently studying abroad at RMIT University in Melbourne. Growing up, I was surrounded by the worlds of business management, marketing, and economics, but over time I felt drawn toward a more creative path. Choosing Digital Design was a leap of faith, but it also felt like the right way to explore my curiosity and passion through a different medium.

One of my current favourite emojis is the squid emoji, 🦑. I honestly do not fully know why, but it randomly came to mind one day and has somehow stayed with me ever since.

In terms of software, Adobe Premiere Pro is one of the tools I enjoy using the most. I have always been interested in video editing, whether for more polished work or smaller fun projects with friends. I also have some prior coding experience from Interactive Media and Interface Design and Development, and I am interested in continuing to develop my understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
